About Me

Hello! I’m Aldi Nurmawan Saputra, a passionate web developer and freelancer based in Indonesia. I specialize in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites and web applications.

With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and frameworks like Bootstrap and Laravel, I help clients bring their digital ideas to life. I love crafting clean code, solving complex problems, and continuously learning new technologies to stay ahead in the ever-evolving web development industry.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked with various startups and companies on projects such as portfolio websites, e-commerce platforms, and business landing pages. My goal is always to create engaging user experiences and deliver results that exceed expectations.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring new tech trends, reading, or collaborating with fellow developers on open-source projects. If you’re looking for a reliable web developer to help grow your online presence, feel free to get in touch!
